About Us

NDYWET-Fund was formed specifically as a social development model to support and spearhead the Youths and Womens Empowerment and social capital investment project in the Niger Delta region with a view of replication in other part of the country.
The main objective of the FUND is to empower young people in the Niger Delta States in particular and Nigeria in general, and to impact on their communities by investing in grassroots, community projects and, the Fund will offer educational grants, business support financing, skills acquisition training; the Fund will also provides funding for other lives touching projects in various communities led by youth and women faith based organizations.

The Fund will always support sustainable social enterprises and provide real time solutions to local challenges and also to reduce the unemployment in the Niger Delta region, as that will further advance the Global Goals for Sustainable Development in local communities across Nigeria in General.

NDYWET-Fund, is about investing in children education, youths, womens empowerment and supporting of windows and elders in the Niger Delta States and by extension Nigeria, in making a living worthful and securing a future for the people and their communities.

NDTWET-Fund, also involves in Economic and Social capital investments, such as business start-ups and community based improvement projects, devised and managed by youths and womens community based group of the Niger Delta region.
NDYWET-Fund projects also involve expert mentors with professional background in the respective areas that needed to tackle the youths and women restiveness. The Fund is one of the flagship projects of Vision 2030, under the social pillar investment.

Its strategic focus is on enterprise and human capacity development project as a key strategy that will increase economic opportunities for, and participation by Niger Delta Youths and Women in nation building. The Fund also seeks to create more employment opportunities for young people through entrepreneurship and encouraging them to be job creators and not job seekers. It does this by providing easy and affordable financial and business development support services to youths and women who are keen on starting up or expanding their businesses. The goal of the Fund is to institutionalize access to resources for youth and women empowerment in Niger Delta in particular and Nigeria in general.

Our Objectives

The object of the Fund is to supplement the provision of education at all levels for all the Niger Delta people in Nigeria. For the purpose of attaining this object, the monies from the Fund are to be expended as follows:

  • To provide financial support for the development and maintenance of academic facilities and infrastructure at primary, high-school and tertiary institutions
  • To offer Scholarship to brilliant but needy Niger Delta students in primary, secondary-cycle and accredited tertiary institutions in Nigeria and across the world
  • To undertake research and other academic programmes of relevance to national development
  • To provide financial support for development of healthcare infrastructure and provision of life-line medical assistance to needy Nigerians.
  • To develop programmes of action for general alleviation of poverty in the Niger Delta region and by extension Nigeria.

    GOVERNANCE

    The governance of the Niger Delta Youth and Women Empowerment Trust Fund will follows the global rules and procedures for universal trust funds managers. There are more than 1.8 billion young people on the planet the largest population in history. They represent a tremendous outlet of ideas and inspiration, a source of leadership and creativity, and a powerful engine for driving sustainable development goal forward. At the same time, they face challenges. Young people are disproportionately affected by poverty and social exclusion, and their ability to participate in development processes is limited. In response to this, the Niger Delta Youth and Women Empowerment Trust Fund is fully aims to contribute to the development of young people, empowering them to come up with solutions to the most pressing challenges of our day and equipping them with the skills and knowledge, as that will put their ideas into action. The initiative also aims to bring the voice of the youth and women into the entrepreneurship development discourse and to help them in realizing their full social, economic and human potential. The Trust Fund, will equally serve as a vital mechanism for supporting youth and women worldwide, by transforming lives through the Fund.
